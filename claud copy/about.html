Our Story

Built by small business owners,
for small business owners

BillMate started with a simple frustration: billing software was either too expensive, too complicated, or made for big enterprises. So we built something better.

Our Mission

Empowering every business, no matter the size

We believe every shop owner, freelancer, and trader deserves professional tools — without needing a CA or an IT team.

"A kirana store owner in Nagpur should have the same billing power as a corporate in Mumbai. That's what we're building."

BillMate was founded in 2023 by a team who spent years watching small business owners struggle with handwritten bills, missed payments, and stressful tax seasons. We set out to change that with software that's genuinely simple, genuinely affordable, and genuinely useful.

Today, over 2,400 businesses across India use BillMate to generate professional invoices, track GST, manage customers, and grow with confidence.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped us

From a weekend side project to thousands of businesses across India — here's how BillMate grew.

January 2023
The idea is born

Arjun, frustrated by a ₹12,000/year billing tool, writes the first 200 lines of BillMate on a Saturday afternoon.

April 2023
First 10 users

We share a WhatsApp link with shop owners in our neighbourhood. 10 of them start using it. Real feedback begins.

August 2023
GST automation launched

The most-requested feature lands. Auto-GST calculation across all product categories. Users love it.

January 2024
500 businesses milestone

Word of mouth takes over. 500 shops across Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Gujarat are now on BillMate.

July 2025
2,400+ businesses. Growing daily.

Team grows to 8. New features: analytics dashboard, PDF downloads, multi-user support. The journey continues.
